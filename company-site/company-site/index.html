Welcome to SGC Design

Welcome to SGC Design, here you can find three web developers and designers who are able to create and design web pages for any business or client. We all have experience in using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to develop a web page that is up to date and meets current web standards. We also have experience working with clients and our projects that were made for them are displayed on this site. There is always more to learn in this industry so we are constantly challenging ourselves and trying to learn different things that clients would want on their website.
